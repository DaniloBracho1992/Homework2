About me

My name is Danilo Bracho, I was born on August 20, 1992. I was born in the city of Maracaibo, Venezuela. At 22 years of age he graduated from the University of Zulia where he studied political science. At 23 I made the decision to move to the United States. I currently live in the city of Chicago and have a part time job as a waiter. Among my most common hobbies are playing videos, seeing new cities and visiting new restaurants in the city.
